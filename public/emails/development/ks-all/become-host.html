What is a Knowledge Session?

A Knowledge Session is a one-on-one virtual interaction between a Host and an Attendee discussing a topic of interest. Some popular topics can include Networking, Mock Interviews, Coding, Academic Journey, and so on.

You can help someone advance in life with your unique experiences by becoming a Host!

Follow these easy steps to create your first Knowledge Session today!

Step 1: Perform Knowledge Session Onboarding.

Step 2: Update availability and provide meeting preferences

Step 3: Submit and wait for our approval within 24-hours!

Once approved, your Knowledge Session will be live and ready for your network to share and schedule direct meetings with you!
